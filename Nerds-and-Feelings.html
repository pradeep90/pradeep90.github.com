Nerds and Feelings

Thoughts

(Very, very rough thoughts - pinch of salt recommended)

Feelings in nerds are not first-class objects.

You can maybe deal with feelings in places, but you can’t talk about them. You can’t explicitly notice what feelings the other person has right now.

They aren’t first-class objects, so you tend to shape your life so that you don’t deal with them at all. And you suffer accordingly.
