The Compare and Contrast Effect

How do you make a rod shorter without touching it?

Place a l***er rod next to it.

What might a Real Movie Experience look like?

  • Watch a bunch of “highly rated” movies one after the other without any distinction

  • Watch each movie separately with lots of build-up and Tender Love and Care

What is a Great Movie?

  • One that has 5 stars from nearly all reviewers

  • One that has 5 stars from your favourite reviewer

  • One that actually gives you the entertainment you want

Is our experience of a movie independent of any factors apart from the movie?

  • Yup. A great movie is a great movie no matter how you watch it.

  • Nope. Our state of mind, the Compare and Contrast effect, Paralysis by Analysis, etc. all matter.
