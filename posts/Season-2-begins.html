Season 2 Begins

I’m Back

And it all comes back in a flash.

I will post as much as I can without endangering my hands. (They’re much better than before, thanks for asking.)

Lots of interesting stuff going on. So much to write about. So much more to figure out and clarify. So many more Big Problems to tackle.

Picking up where we left

Previously, on SPK’s Lame Rationality Training Ground (this website), we had built things up to a crescendo with posts about some Big (and not so Big) Problems.

Memory, Will Power and the hidden Gang-Leader responsible for Akrasia, Learning really fast, and, of course, seeking out the Truth quickly and effectively. I ran across some other Big Problems that I hadn’t considered at all, but I guess that is the way of the world. Will write about that stuff too.

Thinking about things in terms of their Impact, dealing with the problem of To-Do Lists, and “Great Ideas” that go unimplemented. Plus, a bunch of posts on my Sleep problems and my proposed solutions.

I will touch upon all of the important stuff above and lead off into uncharted territory.

Changes

Plenty has changed in the three months I haven’t posted.

I’m posting from an entirely different country. I’m living in a completely different situation. I’m going through some pretty drastic changes in my view of the world and it’s all happening in a matter of weeks.

I’m more optimistic about some things after some experiences over the last three months. In other areas, I’m sadder but wiser after getting bludgeoned around by life.

Bertram Wooster: “Under the tiddly-poms of whatever-it-is… How does the rest of it go?”

Jeeves: “Under the bludgeonings of chance their heads are… pardon me… bloody but unbowed, sir.”

Bertram Wooster: “That’s right. Your own?”

Jeeves: “No, sir. The late William Ernest Henley, 1849-1903.”

All-new Season

A season full of clarity, discovery, mystery-solving, and “Rationality”. Two months of non-stop posts about the finer things in life… and much more.

The Great 300-Word Experiment is back on, bitches.

XOXO, Gossip Girl SPK

PS

Can’t believe how hard it was to get to 300 words here. I must really be out of form. Yet another reason why I need to write regularly.
