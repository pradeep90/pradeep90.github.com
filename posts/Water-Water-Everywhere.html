Strategies

If you’re like most people, you’re getting less water than you should be.

How can you make sure you get enough water?

Think about how much water you should be drinking in total and then figure out how much you should be having on an hourly basis.

  • What are the periods of the day where you neglect to drink water regularly?

    • Is it when you’re in class?

    • Is it when you’re on the computer, intently reading some useful post or, perhaps, writing your 300 words for the day?

    • Or is it when you’re on the move?

  • What is the most useful thing you can do to improve your water intake?
